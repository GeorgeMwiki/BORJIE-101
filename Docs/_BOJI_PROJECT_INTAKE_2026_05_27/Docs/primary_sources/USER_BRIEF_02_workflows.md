## Pre licence and obtaining approval

- Arrive at place where the mining undertaking is to take place
- Take coordinates
- Check mining portal if there is a licence, if not you apply to mining licence
- Apply regional mining office
- Insert coordinate and gain access to tools for shaping area needed for undertaking
- Insert all requirements: passport, tin, business licence, letter to regional head etc
- Click submit and obtain a control number for paying
- Multiple options to pay
- Get access to automatically generated letter of introduction to district as investor and licence as according to mining authority templates with stamps and visible fingerprints, signatures, easy sharing and download options

## Post licence and approval

- Road negotiations with locals and local government
- Enter details and generate Approval documents from local government with appropriate fingerprint authorizations
- Record payments and agreements with appropriate owners where the road crosses
- Appropriate schedule tracking and adjustments. Whole process of road clearing can take 3 days with one excavator

### Determination or research phase

- Allow for Local approach tracking and recording e.g tools used, payments made etc
- Allow for tagging e.g local advisory or geologist advisory
- Most people take local approach by using a hand tools to expose the vein then do guesswork from there
- If a geologist is hired, he will use the same data or observations like the local miners. The geology essentially provides a more intuitive intelligent guess work
- Professional
- Allow for sample recording and cshedule concentration checking from a list of near by available labs to understand level of commerciality
- Appropriate recording of bore hole observed layers. Ability to add diferrent bore holes with nifferent layer, set them across scanned and geofenced area to get trend of veins
- Data fed include: Volume of overburden, depth at which vein can be obtain from which volume of materials can be estimated. So depth, length and withd of vein to get estimated volume of vein at investigated area.
- Calculate estimated deposit
- Automation of the creation of Official research report whiich is bankable
- Ability to add sections for bank oversight on what will be done with the money, Repayment calculations with interest and satisfactory visibility of the operations in real time including history and all compliance document and processes.

### Operations

#### Planning

- Ability to geo tag the area, use tools to expand the area as well as geo fence and break into sections.
- Ability to see infromation on terrain, topography, local history and directions, ecology and conservation strategies with interractive visualizations
- Ability to breakdown the site into sections: Start area, Camp and storage with appropriate tracking e.g- explosives, labourers etc, toilets, overload dumping site, then add go down deposit and quality control place.
- Option to have process above be forecasted and simulated with ai simply by imputing planned start area
- Simulations will also include expansion depending on vein trend
- Movement of escavators and where they should go next to avoid and site collisions or wasting time moving camps
- Forecasts and simulations of mining approach to avoid any pitfalls
- Forecasts and simulations on amounts of overload removal expected and amount of ore to be expected per day, weeks months, yrs
- Schedule forecasts and simulations depending on real time changes e.g amount escavated per day
- Schedule simulations and forecasts will take into account multimple data sets to come into conclusions for example allerting on speed of overload removal by dumber so that the escavator can move freely at all times without any hinderance, then appropriately set and flag areas on the calendar for scheduling with appropriate authorities for onloading work, scheduling of vehicles, containers etc
- Ability to notify these officials directly on the platfrom and record their confirmations for futher plannning
- Real task of scheduling is alligning excavation timing, coordination of material removal, and quality control time, in order to estimate when you should bring in cars and officers for onloading
- Task is also on coordination in such a way that fixed cost on this process remains as low as possible
- Coordination between exaction and processing and onloading into vehicles is very important. Most of the times its so heavy it runs concurrently
- Fore cast of when cargo will reach different cities
- Forecasts on when trucks will drive for pick up
- Estimates on possible demurrage costs
- Money saved
- Time saved
- Orders met on time or under time or over time
- Appropriately segemented cost section with forecasts and simulations
- From fuel, to mantainance, to paying for licence etc
- Appropriate tax and accounting section with simulations and forecasts
